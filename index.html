Biography

 I received my Ph.D degree from the Institute of Intelligence Science and Technology, Hohai University in 2023. My advisor was Prof. Xiaoqin Zeng. I am a lecturer in Computer and information engineering college, Chuzou University. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Graph Neural Networks
  • Action Recognition
