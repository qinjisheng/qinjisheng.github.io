Biography

 I received my Ph.D degree from the Institute of Intelligence Science and Technology, Hohai University in 2023. My advisor was Prof. Xiaoqin Zeng. I am a lecturer in Computer and information engineering college, Chuzou University. Currently, My research focuses on graph intelligence and agent-based modeling for educational scenarios:
  • LLM-based Agent
  • Graph Learning
